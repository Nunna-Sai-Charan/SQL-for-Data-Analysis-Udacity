Quiz Question

Match the appropriate definition or description to each term or column.

1. Has a unique value for every row in that column. There is one in every table.

ans) Primary Key

2. The link to the primary key that exists in another table.

ans) Foreign Key

3. The primary key in every table of our example database.

ans) id

4. A foreign key that exists in both the web_events and orders tables.

ans) account_id

5. The ON statement associated with a JOIN of the web_events and accounts tables.

ans) ON web_events.account_id = accounts.id
